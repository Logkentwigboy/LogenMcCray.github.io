Typography

I have decided to use my fonts from google fonts.com. I wanted to make my website be inviting, but also looking professional. So listed below is the fonts I used. I used Flankin Gothic for the header becuase it is bold and easy to see at the top. I wanted something that was bold like the header, but not as thick, so I selected Motseratt for my fonts. It is a sans serif font that goes well with the website. Lastly I wanted some text that was thin but also professional looking, so I picked Arial, Helvetica for my fonts.


  • Primary Navigation- Franklin Gothic
  • Headers- Motseraat
  • Paragraph- Arial, Helvetica
